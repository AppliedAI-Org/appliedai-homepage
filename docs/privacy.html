Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

Applied AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information across all of our applications, including but not limited to IntelliPromptly, IntelligenceHub, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate and improve our Services, including:

  • Authentication Information: Auth0-provided user identifiers (e.g., Auth0 "sub" IDs).
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as timestamps of access, success/failure of API requests, and general usage metrics.
  • Third-Party Payment Information: Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not collect or store payment information directly.
  • Service-Specific Data: Certain Services, such as IntelligenceHub, allow users to store conversational or application data. This data is stored in user-owned resources (e.g., Azure SQL databases) and is not accessed or collected by us.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as names, mailing addresses, government IDs, or payment card numbers directly through our systems.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Monitor performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze aggregated, anonymized data for service improvement

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.


3. Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party providers to support our Services:

  • Auth0 for authentication and identity management
  • Azure for hosting, telemetry, and database services
  • Stripe for secure payment processing
  • Other integrated APIs and cloud providers necessary to deliver specific features

These third parties are governed by their own privacy policies and data practices.


4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. User-specific data that is part of user-owned resources (e.g., conversational logs stored in your own SQL databases) remains under your control.


5. Data Retention

We retain usage and authentication data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
